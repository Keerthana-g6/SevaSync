# SevaSync: AI-Powered Decision Intelligence for Crisis Response

SevaSync is a robust decision engine designed for the Google Solution Challenge. It transforms unstructured, chaotic field data into a prioritized, predictive, and actionable response plan for NGOs.

**We are not just matching volunteers; we are predicting crises before they escalate.**

## 🌟 The Core Innovation
1. **The Prioritization Engine:** Uses Gemini AI to instantly parse messy field reports, extract critical needs, and rank them by urgency and impact.
2. **Predictive Forecasting:** Anticipates demand spikes and hidden crises (e.g., predicting a disease outbreak based on water shortage reports).
3. **Trust & Transparency Layer:** Features explainable AI that generates a rationale for every decision and flags fairness/coverage gaps.
4. **What-If Simulations:** Allows command centers to stress-test their resource allocations against hypothetical disaster escalations.

## 🚀 Impact Metrics
* **Response Time:** Reduces triage from hours to minutes.
* **Optimization:** Maximizes volunteer utilization by intelligently factoring in location, skills, and priority bands.
